Privacy Policy

Transparency matters to us. Learn how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

The GIST application functions as a secure mobile gateway to your student portal. We collect necessary data to facilitate your academic experience:

  • Authentication: Student ID and Password (for portal login).
  • Academic Metrics: Attendance, Grades, CGPA, and Credits.
  • Identity: Name, Roll Number, and Profile Photograph.
  • Financial: Fee status and payment notifications.

2. Data Usage & Purpose

Your data is processed exclusively to provide student services. We do not use your information for marketing or profiling. The primary uses include:

  • Providing a mobile-optimized view of your academic records.
  • Maintaining persistent login sessions on your device.
  • Notifying you about pending fees or result updates.

3. Security Architecture

We prioritize your data security through a "Local-First" approach:

  • Local Storage: Sensitive credentials are stored only on your device using encrypted Shared Preferences.
  • Direct Connection: The app establishes a direct connection between your device and the official GIST servers. No intermediary servers are used.
  • Data Minimization: We only fetch the data required to display your current academic status.

4. Third-Party Policy

We believe in a clean, ad-free experience. The GIST app contains no trackers, third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), or advertising SDKs. Your data never leaves the official institutional ecosystem.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. Simply logging out of the application or clearing the application cache in your phone settings will remove all locally stored information.
